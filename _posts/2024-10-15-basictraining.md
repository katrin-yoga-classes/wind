Basic Training comprises 40 lessons with the following topics: 

## Asanas

## Energy exercises

- Kriya (Lesson 13-32)
- Pranayama (Lesson 19+)

## Theory

- Yamas (Lesson 1-5)
- Niyamas (Lesson 6-10)
- Asana (Lesson 11)
- Prana (Lesson 12)
- Chakras (Lesson 13-19)
- Pratyahara (Lesson 28-29)
- Yin-Yang model (Lesson 30)
- Kriya Yoga (Lesson 31)
- Kleshas (Lesson 32, 34, 36)
- Sambhavi Mudra (Lesson 39-40)
